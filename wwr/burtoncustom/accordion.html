What is the Burton Custom snowboard?

The Burton Custom snowboard is a versatile all-mountain board designed for adventure-seeking riders who demand performance, durability, and style.

Why choose Burton Custom?

It offers top-notch flexibility, control, and stability, making it perfect for both beginners and experienced snowboarders.

How do I maintain my snowboard?

Keep your board waxed and store it in a dry place. Regular tune-ups and edge maintenance will extend its lifespan.
